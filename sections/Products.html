Find out more at nimble.com

NIMBLE is a building information modeling and behavioral analysis tool. Utilizing model based data, the product graphically presents a powerful series of lifecycle preparedness metrics in an intuitive, browser-based dashboard. NIMBLE leverages this data to expose modeling techniques that would be unfamiliar to the user, augmenting and automating their workflows.
